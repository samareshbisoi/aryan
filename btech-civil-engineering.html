Civil engineering

The Civil engineering is a professional engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment.

The B.Tech program of the Civil Engineering imparts students with mastery in the field of Civil Engineering besides fluency in Mathematics, Physics, Instrumentation, Computes, Management and economics which are quite essential for any successful Civil Engineer. Students gain practical experience from field visits conducted by the department to industries, Dams and Construction sites etc.

Civil Engineers plan, design, and supervise the construction of facilities essential to modern life like space satellites and launching facilities, offshore structures, bridges, buildings, tunnels, highways, transit systems, dams, airports, harbors, water supply system and wastewater treatment plants.

Students are not only made experts in technical aspects but also in interpersonal skills which is highly required to excel in the fast- paced world.The department also runs M.Tech program in “Structural Engineering” since 2014-15 academic sessions.

The academic programmes, B. Tech., M.Tech, of the Civil Engineering department at Aryan are some of the best in Odisha.One of the best institute for Civil engineering provides interdisciplinary academic and research activity.
