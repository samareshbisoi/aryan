Electrical engineering

Electrical engineering is a professional engineering discipline that generally deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism.Aryan Engineering College is having a vibrant atmosphere due to the combination of input of brilliant students, availability of outstanding faculty members and innovative research work in all areas of Electrical Engineering.

Electrical engineering is termed as an evergreen field, and with the rapid pace of industrialisaton, there is more scope than ever. The electrical engineer can work in designing, developing, evaluating, selling, marketing, handling and improving electrical products. Today it is known to be the broadest and the largest engineering discipline.

The faculty in the department is a rich blend of personnel with industrial and professional experience. The department encourages student to undergo industrial training at various organizations for in-depth, practical exposure. The college is fully equipped with Electrical Engineering lab , Network Devices Lab, Electrical Machines Labs, Energy Conversion Devices Lab , Electrical Measurement Lab , Control & Instrumentation Lab , Power Electronics Lab , Electrical Drives Lab , Simulations Lab , Power Systems Lab , Project Lab as per the university syllabus.

One of the Best B.Tech Institute of Bhubaneswar,the main goal of Aryan Engineering College is to Provide the Industry Electrical Engineers, with dynamic well rounded personalities adaptable to ever increasing demands of emerging technologies involving analytical and practical skills.
